Final lesson

The final lesson

Next week is the final lesson for the spring semester.

For the block 3 test please prepare your notes.

 

Block 1 notes

Vocabulary - Animals, Buildings; Family and People; Fruit, Vegetables, and Nuts.

Exercises - U.K. vs U.S. English; Capitals, Italics, Double Quotes; Writing numbers.

 

Block 2 notes

Vocabulary - Household objects; Mathematics; Nature; Occupations.

Exercises - "Have you ever?" vs "Do you ever?"; "Look like", and "Be like"; "Above" and "Over".

 

Block 3 notes

Vocabulary - Occupations; Science; Time, Tools; Transport.

Exercises - Correct Verb Form (動詞の形); Subject - Verb (主語 - 動詞); Singular (単数名詞), Plural (複数の), or Possessive (所有の); Infinitives (不定詞); Prepositions (前置詞).
