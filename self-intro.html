ACADEMIC ENGLISH

 

SELF-INTRODUCTION

 

Write about yourself.

 

How to write

Paragraph 1 - Introduction

Paragraph 2 - About your hobbies, interests, or sports.

Paragraph 3 - About your family.

Paragraph 4 - About your hometown.

250 to 300 words.

 

Scoring

1. The essay must have a title. (2 points)

2. There must be four paragraphs. (8 points)

3. The essay must be saved as a PDF. (2 points)

4. The file must be named: self-introduction-[student code here]. (2 points)
Example - self-introduction-JLA.pdf

5. Submit your essay on the assignment classroom website.
