ACADEMIC ENGLISH

 

MAKE A SURVEY

 

Team survey exercise.

 

About the Survey

You and two other classmates will make a survey together. You can choose from a list of survey topics, or suggest your own topic.

The teacher will assign you to teams, and together you must make a total of 9 questions.

The quiz will be made in Google Forms.

The surveys will be made available to all students to answer in block 2.

An assignment about the survey must be handed-in by week 8.

 

Schedule

Week 2 - Try an example survey, and choose your topic.

Week 3 - Start making a survey.

Week 5 - All surveys will be available on the website to answer.

Week 8 - Hand-in a report about your survey.

 

Scoring

Survey example - 9 points

Survey topic choice - 3 points

Make a survey - 20 points

Survey assignment - 80 points
